Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 18, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 18, 2026  ·  Jurisdiction: State of Ohio, United States

Summary: ViCatus collects health and usage data solely to provide and improve its services. We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data with third parties except as necessary to operate the app. Your health data is yours.

1. Who We Are

ViCatus ("we," "us," "our") is a mobile health and fitness application operated by an individual developer based in Columbus, Ohio, United States. The app is accessible via iOS and Android platforms and the website vicatus.com. For privacy inquiries, contact us at support@vicatus.com.

2. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the ViCatus mobile application, the ViCatus website (vicatus.com), and any related services. By using ViCatus, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, do not use the app or website.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information: name, email address, password
  • Health profile: age, gender, height, current weight, goal weight
  • Food and meal entries you log or describe within the app
  • Workout and exercise entries you log within the app
  • Weight measurements you manually enter
  • Any communications you send to us (support emails, feedback)

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Step count and movement data via your device's built-in sensors (accelerometer, pedometer)
  • Device information: device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • App usage data: features used, session duration, frequency of use
  • Log data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time and date of visits
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data

3.3 Information from Third-Party Services

  • Payment processing data via RevenueCat (subscription status, purchase history — we do not store full payment card details)
  • Nutritional and recipe data processed via Rork AI API based on your meal descriptions
  • Product listings and pricing data from retail marketplace partners (Amazon, Walmart, Target, GNC, iHerb, Vitamin Shoppe, and others)

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the ViCatus app and its features
  • To calculate your calorie deficit, TDEE, real fat loss, and projected weight
  • To generate AI-powered meal suggestions and workout logs personalized to your goals
  • To process subscription payments and manage your account
  • To display relevant health products in the marketplace
  • To improve, debug, and optimize the app based on usage patterns
  • To respond to your support requests and communications
  • To send you service-related notifications (not marketing, unless you opt in)
  • To comply with legal obligations

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: RevenueCat (payment processing), Rork AI (nutritional AI processing), hosting and infrastructure providers — solely to operate the app
  • Marketplace partners: When you click on a product in the marketplace, you are redirected to a third-party retailer. We may share a session identifier or referral code. We do not share your health data with retailers.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred. You will be notified before any such transfer occurs
  • Protection of rights: To protect the rights, property, or safety of ViCatus, our users, or the public

6. Health Data — Special Protections

Important: ViCatus collects sensitive health information including weight, calorie intake, and physical activity. This data is used exclusively to provide the app's core functionality. It is never sold, never used for advertising targeting, and never shared with insurance companies, employers, or any party that could use it to your detriment.

We apply additional safeguards to health data including encryption at rest and in transit, strict access controls, and minimal data retention periods.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing support@vicatus.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days. Some data may be retained longer where required by law or for legitimate business purposes such as fraud prevention.
